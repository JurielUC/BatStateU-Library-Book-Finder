Library Book Finder Library Policy Do's and Don'ts inside the library Do's Do make use of the Library, the treasure of knowledge. Do switch your mobile to silent Please sign in the visitors’ register each time the user enters the library Please carry ID card to the library to produce when needed Please return issued books in time to avoid overdue charges Please work with your friends on homework or projects if you need to, but please do it quietly so as not to disturb others who visit library for the same purpose. Please treat library with respect, and leave it clean and orderly Please save your files , as computers sometimes freeze and your work will be lost if you don't save it first Use Library PCs only for research/academic works Do ask for help if you need when using computers. Always ask for what you want Respect Library Rules and Regulations Use bins Use book markers Do leave the books on table after reference work is completed It's your Library, so DO respect Library property and do your part to keep library neat and clean.

Don'ts Do not bring food or drink into the library Don't make voice calls Do not use pen drive on systems provided in CDC(Library) Please do not carry other library/personal books Do not play games on computers Do not come in at the last minute and expect to get everything you need. You might be disappointed. It's much better to plan ahead, and set up a timeline for your work in library. Don't disturb others around you Do not sleep inside the Library Do not talk loudly or walk noisily inside the Library Don’t damage any library property and no pilferage Don't write anything in library books. Don’t keep the books in racks after reading Do not discuss inside library Do not try to keep the books in racks
